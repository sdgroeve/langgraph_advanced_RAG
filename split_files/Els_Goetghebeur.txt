Name: Els Goetghebeur
Unit: Statistics
Bio: Els Goetghebeuris Full Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics, Computer Science and Statistics.  She chairs the UGent expert Center for Statistics.  She is chief-editor of ‘Statistics in Medicine’ and chair of the causal inference group of STRATOS‘Strengthening Analytical Thinking for Observational Studies’. Her research is devoted to the development and application of data analytic methods for primarily biomedical applications generally and with a focus on causal inference, survival analysis and quality of care.
Keywords: Biostatistics, causal inference, quality of care, survival analysis
